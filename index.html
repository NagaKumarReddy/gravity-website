About Gravity

A 2-tier organization started on 10th October 2019, growing exponentially with creative events that build confidence in participants.

Our Origin Story

Like Newton, an apple made it happen. The idea struck when a member realized students needed recognition for unconventional talents - even binge-watching web series!

"What if watching web series would acquire me a certificate? After all, it needs a lot of patience!"

Our Mission

To create platforms where people realize everyone is good at something, building confidence through participation in their areas of interest.

"Many lack confidence, not skill. Confidence is the key."
